Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti is always a hit! A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and everyone loved it. Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests. I have made this also without the meat, and it is well received.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 Â½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Gather all ingredients.

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add ziti pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 minutes; drain.

  3. Step 3

    4. Meanwhile, brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 15 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Butter a 9x13-inch baking dish.

  4. Step 4

    5. Spread 1/2 of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish.

  5. Step 5

    6. Top with Provolone cheese.

  6. Step 6

    7. Top with sour cream.

  7. Step 7

    8. Add 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, mozzarella cheese, and remaining meat sauce. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.

  8. Step 8

    9. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted, about 30 minutes.
